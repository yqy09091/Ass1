About Chinese Orchestra

Welcome to CO website are you looking forward for more events about chinese orchestra? Or want to learn more knowledge aboout tradition instruments, you are coming to the right place!

Our orchestra have more than 300 professional members in our 4 main sections and we are getting the Best Chinese Orchestra award for the year2018 and 2021. At the same time we are also looking forward for new memebers. If you are interest in joining us please click join us to look forward for more detail.

Last but not least hope you can get the information you want from our website and have fun in CO.
